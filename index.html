What to compare. All four clips run the identical policy. ball_x_pos and low_fast reach 3 raw gates with a window pass but end in a crash; ball_y_pos stays upright (no crash) but only clears 1 gate. This is a verified partial-credit policy — no scenario completes all four gates, windows, and target dwell. The launch that earns the early gates over-energizes the ball (~3.9 m apex), which the back-half planner cannot convert into gates 3-4.
