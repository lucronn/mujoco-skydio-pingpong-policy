Policy Performance Progression

Progression Summary

🚀 Epoch 0-15: Exploration

Initial nominal policies (U1-U3) commanded high collective thrust. Average of 2.75 raw gates and 2.0 bounced gates, but 100% crash rates due to severe attitude tilt recovery spikes (up to 180°).

🛡️ Epoch 16-30: Safety Focus

Attitude tilt limited strictly to <70°. Collision rates dropped significantly, securing stable flight paths and a peak safety score of 0.451 at iteration 26.

🎯 Epoch 31-49: Window Threading

Prioritized controlled, low-velocity transits through vertical window targets. Reached perfect 1.0 window pass averages and a final optimal score of 0.142.
